## Verläufe: scatter was invisible + still slow — the trajectory chart is rebuilt on Plotly (J2/K1/K2 follow-up, HIGH)

**You wrote:** "Scatter points are not displayed at all anymore." / "Performance still poor, very slow loading of plots, scatter-plots are missing, UI unresponsive when loading." / "The option to zoom in and out or select sections (as in plotly) would be helpful."

**What we found:** you were right, and our v1.16 fix was at fault. The canvas we introduced never sized its drawing surface, so it painted **zero pixels** — the points were genuinely gone — and the old per-point chart nodes were still being built underneath, so it was *also* still slow. We did not catch it because our check confirmed the canvas existed without confirming it actually drew anything. That check has been fixed.

**What changed:** the cohort trajectory chart (Visus & CRT, all three panels) has been **rebuilt on Plotly.js with WebGL scatter**. This removes the thousands of chart DOM nodes entirely (the real cause of the slowness across rounds) and draws the points on the GPU.
- **Scatter points are visible again** — and we no longer cap them at 1,500: all points render (e.g. 1,836 OD / 1,729 OS / 3,565 combined).
- **Settings changes are responsive** — toggling a layer went from ~10 s to ~1 s in our (software-rendered) test harness, and will be faster still on your machine's GPU.
- **Zoom / pan / box-select / lasso** are now available directly on the plot (the toolbar at the top-right of each chart), plus autoscale/reset — the Plotly-style interaction you asked for.
- The single, layer-aware tooltip and click-to-open-case behaviour from v1.16 are preserved (scatter point on hover when Streupunkte is on; line highlight + patient tooltip when only the curves are on).

**How to verify:** Analyse → Verläufe on all 245 → enable "Streupunkte": dense point clouds appear and the view stays responsive while you toggle layers; drag to box-zoom, use the toolbar to pan/reset.

**Status:** ✅ Fixed (rebuilt on Plotly/WebGL; scatter visible, responsive, zoomable).

## "Kohorten vergleichen" locked after deselecting sub-cohorts (K7 follow-up, HIGH)

**You wrote:** "Problem remains unchanged. After unselecting the sub-cohorts, I can not make any selection in the menu."

**What changed:** the compare selector discarded the whole selection whenever it dropped below two cohorts, which left it stuck with nothing selectable. It now keeps any non-empty selection, so you can deselect down to one and freely pick others; the overlay simply activates once two or more are selected.

**How to verify:** Analyse → "Kohorten vergleichen" → deselect everything → you can select cohorts again one by one; nothing is greyed out.

**Status:** ✅ Fixed.

## Cohort overlay: legend, tooltips, IOD chart type, axis (K3 follow-ups)

**You wrote (several):** IQR legend missing in the relative Visus/CRT line plot; IQR legend colour equals the patient-curve colour instead of the transparent band; tooltips differ between absolute and relative plots; the IOD plot is a bar chart unlike Visus/CRT; calendar dates expected when the overlay is off, relative time when it's on.

**What changed:**
- **IQR legend (L4a/L4b):** the Visus **and** CRT cohort-IQR bands now both have a legend entry, and the legend swatch is drawn in the **actual band colour and transparency** (not the opaque curve colour), so it matches what's on the plot. Same correction on the IOD plot.
- **Tooltip parity (L4c):** the absolute and relative plots now show the **same tooltip** — measurements plus the cohort median — and no longer surface raw IQR-band values.
- **IOD as a line plot (L4d):** the IOD/Augeninnendruck cohort overlay is now a **median line + IQR band**, consistent with the Visus and CRT plots (no longer bars over time).
- **Dynamic X-axis (L5):** the individual trajectory now shows **calendar dates when the cohort overlay is off**, and switches to **relative "Monate seit Erstvisite" when the overlay is on** — exactly as you described. IVI markers and the click-highlight follow whichever axis is active.

**How to verify:** open a case → X-axis shows calendar dates; tick "Kohorten-Referenz anzeigen" → the axis switches to relative months, both Visus and CRT IQR bands have a matching-colour legend swatch, the IOD plot shows a median line + band, and the tooltips read the same on both axes.

**Status:** ✅ Fixed (a/b/c/d + axis).

## Injections, markers, interpolation on the trajectory (K3d / further reports)

**You wrote:** injections clickable in the list but not in the top "Behandlungsverlauf" time series; "Offene Kreise" are back; interpolated values sometimes don't sit on the line.

**What changed:**
- **L6:** injections are now **clickable in the top Behandlungsverlauf strip** as well (where the measurements are clickable) — clicking highlights the injection in the plot and the list; a click elsewhere or navigating to another case clears it.
- **L7:** the stray **open-circle markers are removed** — markers are filled and consistent again.
- **L8:** the separate interpolated-point series was the culprit; interpolated gaps are now drawn **through the line itself** (no marker floating off the curve), so the line is unambiguous.

**How to verify:** open a case → in the top strip, click an injection: it highlights (and in the list); the trajectory shows filled markers only, with interpolated segments lying on the line.

**Status:** ✅ Fixed.

## Dark mode (Further Remarks, new)

**You wrote:** "When my system setting is bright mode, the dashboard still displays 'Zum Hellen Modus wechseln'… the dashboard should adapt the default to my system settings and display all figures and tables in dark mode when activated."

**What changed:**
- **Default follows your OS (L11a):** the theme now defaults to **"System"** (your `prefers-color-scheme`), so a light OS opens light and a dark OS opens dark out of the box. The toggle still lets you pin Light or Dark explicitly (it cycles Light → Dark → System).
- **Full coverage (L11b):** all charts and tables — the trajectory plots, the cohort overlay, the IOD/distribution charts, the patient header/timeline, the quality table — now restyle for dark mode (axes, gridlines, text, legend, bands, markers), not just the page chrome.

**How to verify:** set your OS to dark (or pick "System"/"Dunkel" via the toggle) → the whole dashboard, including every figure and table, renders in dark.

**Status:** ✅ Fixed.
