## v1.9.5 Scope: Synthetic Data Realism (SYNTH-*)

Source: post-v1.9.4 audit of bundle coverage against terminology seed + clinical realism review (2026-04-30).

- [x] **SYNTH-01** — `_seedMap` in `src/services/terminology.ts` covers all 5 currently-unresolvable diagnoses present in `public/data/center-*.json`: SNOMED `312903003` (DME, 51 cases), SNOMED `362098006` (RVO, 24 cases), ICD-10-GM `E11` (T2DM unspecified, 21 cases), `H43.1` (vitreous hemorrhage, 2 cases), `T85.8` (post-injection IOP elevation, 3 cases). Audit script reports 0 unresolvable.
  **Outcome:** ✅ VALIDATED — `_seedMap` grew from 10 to 15 entries. `audit:bundles` exits 0 (6 bundles, 31 codes, 0 unresolvable). Drift-guard test enforces symmetry at every CI run. (Phase 26-01)

- [x] **SYNTH-02** — `scripts/generate-center-bundle.ts` emits disease-conditional comorbidities: ≥60% AMD patients gain ≥1 of {`I10`, `E78.0`, `I25.1`} (age-correlated); 100% DR/DME patients have a diabetes Condition (`E10.9` or `E11.9`); ≥40% DR/DME patients also have `I10`.
  **Outcome:** ✅ VALIDATED — AMD comorbidity 65% ≥ 60%, DME diabetes 100%, DME I10 ~40%. (Phase 26-02)

- [x] **SYNTH-03** — DR/DME synthetic patients receive 2–5 HbA1c (LOINC `4548-4`) observations per case (baseline 6.5–10.5 %, plausible glycemic-control trajectories). AMD onset restricted to age ≥60. AMD/DME/RVO templates differentiated (CRT levels, IVI count distribution, laterality preference).
  **Outcome:** ✅ VALIDATED — HbA1c 100% emission for DME (2–5 obs, baseline 7.5–10.5%), AMD median age 74 (range 60–91), TEMPLATES constant differentiates IVI/CRT/drugs/laterality per cohort. Faricimab (S01LA09) and Dexamethasone (S01BA01) added. (Phase 26-03)

- [x] **SYNTH-04** — `npm run generate-bundles` regenerates 4 synthetic site bundles. Quick-check script confirms aggregate priors (AMD median age ≥70, DR comorbidity rate 100%, AMD comorbidity rate ≥60%, HbA1c emission rate per DR/DME case ≥2). `npm run test:ci` stays green ≥640 (±5 churn allowance).
  **Outcome:** ✅ VALIDATED — 4 bundles regenerated atomically. verify-bundle-distributions.mjs confirms all priors. 682/682 tests green (well above ≥640 floor). (Phase 26-04)
